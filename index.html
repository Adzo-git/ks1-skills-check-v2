A gentle maths check for your child

About 37 short questions, taking roughly 12–18 minutes. There’s no timer and no pass or fail — it simply helps us see what your child is confident with and what to explore next.

Before we begin

Ready, there?

Here’s how it works. You can read these together.

  • 1You’ll see one maths question at a time. Tap the answer you think is right.
  • 2It’s okay if you’re not sure — just have a go and try your best.
  • 3There’s no clock and no rush. Take all the time you need to think.
  • 4When you’ve chosen, tap Next to move on.
A note for the grown-up: there’s no time limit, so please let your child answer independently rather than prompting them. If they’re not sure, a sensible guess is completely fine — it’s all useful information. The aim of this check is to see what your child knows right now, not to get a perfect score.
